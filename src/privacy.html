Privacy Policy

Introduction

At Solace, we take your privacy seriously. This policy describes how we collect, use, and protect your personal information when you use our ADHD video platform.

Information We Collect

  • Account information (email, username)
  • Authentication data (securely handled by Firebase)
  • User preferences and settings
  • Video interaction history
  • Playlist and watch later data
  • Comments and ratings

Data Security

Your security is our priority. We use industry-standard security measures:

  • Firebase Authentication with secure password hashing
  • Encrypted data transmission
  • Secure database storage
  • Regular security audits

How We Use Your Data

We use your information to:

  • Provide and improve our services
  • Personalize your experience
  • Maintain and optimize platform performance
  • Send important notifications
  • Analyze usage patterns to improve features

Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services:

  • Firebase Authentication and Database for secure user data management
  • YouTube Data API v3 for video content

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your personal data
  • Request data correction or deletion
  • Opt-out of non-essential communications
  • Export your data

For privacy concerns, contact us at: privacy@solace.com
