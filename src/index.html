FAQ

Common Questions

Find answers to frequently asked questions about our video library.

What type of content is available in the library?

Our library features a diverse collection of educational videos about ADHD, including management strategies, educational content, expert interviews, and practical tips. Content is categorized by topic, difficulty level, and age group.

How is the content organized?

Videos are organized into categories including Education, Strategies, Lifestyle, and Medical Information. You can filter by topic, duration, rating, and more to find exactly what you need.

Can I save videos to watch later?

Yes! Once registered, you can save videos to your "Watch Later" list, create custom playlists, and track your viewing progress.

How often is new content added?

I regularly update our library with new content. Using Google Youtube Data API v3, videos are updated weekly directly from Youtube.
