Hi, I'm Julian

The creator of Solace. As a high school student living with ADHD, I understand the unique challenges and strengths it brings.

My journey with ADHD led me to create Solace, a platform offering the support and tools I wish I had when I was first diagnosed at age 8. From battling endless procrastination to needing constant reminders for chores, ADHD has been my constant companion.

My mission is to empower every person with ADHD to thrive, not just survive. Through Solace, I aim to foster a world where ADHD is understood, accepted, and celebrated for the unique perspectives it brings.
