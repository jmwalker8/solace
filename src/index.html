Frequently asked questions.

What is Solace?

+

Solace is a comprehensive platform designed to support individuals with ADHD through personalized strategies, community support, and innovative tools.

How can Solace help me manage my ADHD?

+

Solace provides personalized strategies, productivity tools, expert-led webinars, and a supportive community to help you better manage your ADHD symptoms and improve your daily life.

What tools and resources does Solace offer?

+

Solace offers a range of tools including customizable task managers, focus timers, reminder systems, and educational resources tailored for individuals with ADHD.

Is Solace suitable for both adults and children with ADHD?

+

Yes, Solace provides resources and support for both adults and children with ADHD, with age-appropriate content and tools available for different user groups.

How do I join the Solace community?

+

You can join the Solace community by clicking the 'Get started' button and following the sign-up process. Once registered, you'll have access to our forums, resources, and tools.
